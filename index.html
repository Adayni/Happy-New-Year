Welcome, 2021

A new beginning
A clean slate
A fresh start

As we embark upon 2021, I just want to let you know that this next year cannot be "business as usual".
If it's one thing we've learnt from 2020, it's the fragility of life. We cannot afford to play with the time that we have. The time we've been granted so far is God's grace and mercy to give us just a bit more time to get ready.
For 2021, I pray we place God first in our lives.
I pray we ask Him daily for the willingness and the strength to follow His will.
I pray that when He returns at His Second Coming that we are saved in His Kingdom.
It is my prayer that we get it together.
That we make our calling and election sure.
That we gain the victory over every sin and lay aside the weights that hold us back from serving God fully.
Nothing short of perfection will get us into God's Kingdom. We cannot afford to play with our salvation. The time is far spent. We are in the closing scenes of earth's history.

Let us enter into 2021 with a new mindset.
A mindset that is focused - Focused on God.
Focused on what we are called to be and to do as His ambassadors; as His representatives; as His children.
This is my prayer for us for 2021.
